Books
  • Machine Learning Yearning by Andrew Ng
    Description: A valuable resource authored by the renowned Andrew Ng. This book provides in-depth insights into machine learning strategies and practical approaches. It's a must-read for those looking to excel in machine learning.
  • Designing Data-Intensive Applications by Martin Kleppmann
    Description: A gold standard in the field of Data Engineering, Database Systems, and Parallel and Distributed Computing. This book provides a deep dive into the inner workings of data tools like Elasticache, databases, and more. It's a 5/5 rating for its reliability and maintainability insights.
  • Designing Machine Learning Systems: A Guide for MLOps and Data Engineering
    Description: This book not only covers the technical aspects but also explains MLOps, offering insights into real-world data challenges such as class imbalance, data leakage, and data shift monitoring. It's a must-read for understanding the practical aspects of Machine Learning.
  • Machine Learning System Design by Roche and Andriy Burkov
    Description: Explore how machine learning models are applied to various use cases with practical examples. This book is an excellent resource for gaining insights into the real-world applications of machine learning.
  • System Design Interview: An Insider's Guide by Alex Xu
    Description: Regarded as one of the best comprehensive system design books available. It offers practical insights into system design, making it an invaluable resource for those looking to design robust and scalable systems.

AI Influential Figures to Follow

  • Andrew Ng (Twitter): Co-founder of Google Brain, co-founder of Coursera, and a renowned AI researcher known for his contributions to deep learning and online education.
  • Yann LeCun (Twitter): Chief AI Scientist at Facebook, Turing Award winner, and a pioneer in convolutional neural networks (CNNs).
  • Andrej Karpathy (Twitter): Director of AI and Autopilot Vision at Tesla and a former Stanford researcher known for his work on computer vision and deep learning.
  • Geoffrey Hinton (Twitter): Another Turing Award winner and a trailblazer in deep learning, particularly for his work on neural networks and backpropagation.
  • Demis Hassabis (Twitter): Co-founder of DeepMind, which has made significant contributions to AI research, including AlphaGo.
  • Ian Goodfellow (Twitter): A key figure in the development of generative adversarial networks (GANs) and author of the book "Deep Learning."
  • Gary Marcus (Twitter): AI researcher and author who writes about the limitations and future directions of AI.
  • Fei-Fei Li (Twitter): Co-director of the Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute and an advocate for ethical AI and AI for social good.
  • Lex Fridman (Twitter): MIT researcher, podcast host, and AI enthusiast who explores various AI topics and interviews experts in the field.
  • Joanna Bryson (Twitter): AI researcher and advocate for AI ethics and responsible AI development.
